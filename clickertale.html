Clickertale 3 is the sequel to the Clickertale series, an Undertale fangame in which Chara and their allies must defeat an entity known as ClickGaster who's main goal is to destroy their universe following the unintended destruction of his own. Clickertale 3 will bring to you new story, characters, mechanics, enemies, and bosses while reducing the original 'clicker game' gameplay mechanic, leaving you with a new world to explore following the events in the Void of Clickertale 2.
